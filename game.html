Welcome to the world of Avatar.
You need to find a pair for each card. Find all 10 pairs and become the winner.
But... 21 cards right there? Why another one?

Good question my friend. Among the 20 cards there is one more - a special card. If you find all 10 pairs, and then open a special card, the game will tell you that you have won. If you open a special card before you match all the pairs, then... you lose.

Good luck! And onward to adventure! Hip-Hip!
